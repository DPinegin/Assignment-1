About Website

Hello! My name is Owner and I made this webiste. This website will be about history, hence the name "Historion". The topics that will be shown here are mostly Ancient History and European history (yes, two very different topics on the same website), with focus on art, culture, mthology/folklore, military, architecture, economy and geography. Fun! I hope you will be confused interested!

"Weekly-Study" is a weekly study about an ancient civilization. Every time there will be an introduction about the civilization, geographical location of the civilization, the peak of the civilization, the collapse of the civilization and archaeological findings of famous arrcheologists in that area. The Weekly Study will be updated every week, and on every 4th of the month the continents on which the civilization is located will change.

The purpose of this site is to bring more people attention to history, and tell the history of historical civilizations that are not found in the regular school curriculum (unless they are in the school curriculum). I hope you enjoy my work!
